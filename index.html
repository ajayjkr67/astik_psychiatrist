About

Dr. Astik Mane is an experienced psychiatrist with over 15 years in clinical practice and academia. He focuses on listening carefully, accurate diagnosis, and minimal effective interventions, combining psychotherapy, pharmacotherapy, and CBT.

Qualifications
MBBS (GSMC & KEM), DPM (Central Institute of Psychiatry), DNB Psychiatry (HBTMC).
Practice Areas
Mood disorders, Anxiety, OCD, Psychosis, CBT, De-addiction support.
Academic
Assistant Professor (Contractual), Department of Psychiatry, HBT Medical College & Dr. R.N. Cooper Hospital.

Services

Psychiatric Assessment

Comprehensive psychiatric evaluation, diagnosis using standard scales where appropriate, personalised treatment plan.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Structured CBT sessions for anxiety, depression, and OCD with regular progress reviews.

Medication Management

Evidence-based pharmacotherapy with emphasis on minimal effective dosing and monitoring for side effects.

De-addiction Support

Support for nicotine and tobacco de-addiction and relapse prevention strategies.

Crisis Intervention

Acute care planning for mental health emergencies in coordination with hospital services.

Referrals & Tests

Referrals for investigations and collaborative care with specialists where necessary.
